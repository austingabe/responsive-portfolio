About Me

My name is Austin Lee and I am an amateur photographer based in Los Angeles, California. My love of photography began when I was in middle school with my Canon point-and-shoot cameras, and took off when I purchased my first GoPro in 2014 and my first DSLR in 2015. The majority of my photography has been for personal use, whether it is personal enjoyment or for my Instagram feeds. But in recent years, I have been blessed to have the opportunity to volunteer my skills at various events and functions, spanning from fundraising galas to overseas mission trips. I do not take paid gigs regularly, but I am growing my portfolio and am absolutely ready and willing to take on more paid work.
